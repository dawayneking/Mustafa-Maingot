Biography


Greetings, and welcome to my Website. My name is Mustafa Maingot, and I love the water. Working outside has been one of the greatest experiences, in which the people around me always feeds me vital information. With several companies, I worked with cranes and lifting yachts, working on a barge to help with the new construction of seawalls, and several other marine industries. Working at the Port of Palm Beach/Port Everglades has led me to get TWIC Card, and now I am waiting on my clearance from the Coast Gaurd to become a Merchant Mariner - Merchant Mariner Certification (MMC).
